Triage Playbook

The flow below represents the high-level stages of support triage.

  1. Identify: Categorize the ticket (e.g., navigation, editor, attachments, downtime).
  2. Prioritize: Mark urgent cases (HOT tickets, enterprise impact).
  3. Route: Assign to the correct segment or product team.
  4. Enable: Share ready-to-use resources or helper bot responses.
  5. Close the loop: Follow up, confirm resolution, document lessons learned.
